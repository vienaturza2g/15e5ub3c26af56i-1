<p><p>Projectile Looms are advanced weaving machines designed to produce a variety of fabrics, primarily used for heavy and technical textiles. These looms operate by using a projectile to shuttle the weft yarn across the warp threads, allowing for high-speed weaving and the ability to handle complex patterns. Their efficiency and versatility make them popular in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and industrial textiles.</p><p>The Projectile Loom Market is poised for substantial growth, expected to expand at a CAGR of 11.30% during the forecast period.
