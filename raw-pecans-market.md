<p><p>The raw pecans market is primarily divided into two types: in-shell pecans and shelled pecans. In-shell pecans are sold with their hard outer shells intact, appealing to consumers seeking a natural product with a longer shelf life. They are often used for snacking and as decorative elements in culinary applications. Shelled pecans, on the other hand, are sold without their shells and are preferred for baking, cooking, and snacks, providing convenience and ease of use in various recipes.</p></p>
