<p><strong>The Drinking Fountains Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>BWC</li><li>POU</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The drinking fountains market can be categorized into two main types: Bottled Water Coolers (BWC) and Point of Use (POU) systems. BWC units rely on pre-packaged bottled water, offering portability and ease of use, making them ideal for temporary setups and events. In contrast, POU systems connect directly to the water supply, providing a continuous source of filtered water, which is more sustainable and cost-effective for permanent installations. Both types cater to different consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
