<p><strong>The Atomic Magnetometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold atomic magnetometry</li><li>Spin-exchange relaxation-free (SERF) magnetometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The atomic magnetometer market comprises two primary types: cold atomic magnetometers and spin-exchange relaxation-free (SERF) magnetometers. Cold atomic magnetometers operate at ultra-low temperatures, leveraging the properties of cold atoms to achieve high sensitivity in detecting magnetic fields. In contrast, SERF magnetometers utilize a unique interaction between atoms to minimize relaxation effects, allowing for exceptional sensitivity even in room-temperature environments. Both technologies are increasingly utilized in various applications, including biomedical imaging, geological exploration, and navigation systems.</p></p>
