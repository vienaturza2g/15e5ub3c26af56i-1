<p><ul><li>Liquid</li><li>Granules</li><li>Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The maple sugar market encompasses three main types: liquid, granules, and powder. Liquid maple sugar is a syrup-like product that retains moisture, making it ideal for baking and as a sweetener. Granules, on the other hand, are a crystallized form that offers a coarse texture, suitable for sprinkling on foods or incorporating into recipes for added crunch. Powdered maple sugar is finely ground, easily dissolving in beverages and baked goods, providing a concentrated flavor without added moisture.</p></p>
